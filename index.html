Biodiversity

Summary

Biodiversity is the variety of life in the world from smallest to largest. This is really impotant because it helps the ecosystem stay healthy, with high diversity, the ecosystem helps to provide food, clean air, and clean water. It also maintains the climate and the atmosphere.

How is Biodiversity affected?

Biodiversity is affceted from humans or natural disasters. Biodiverstiy is affected from pollution, deforestation, habitat loss. Natural activities like extreme weather, floods, and, volcanic eruptions.
